COMPUTER DIAGNOSTICS

Modern vehicles have multiple on-board computers. It is no longer possible to carry out the full range of repairs and maintenance on your car without being able to work with these computers. To enable us to do this, so that we can accurately diagnose faults and carry out repairs, we have recently purchased the high tech ‘Global Scan’ tool.

This tool is configured for the worldwide ‘CAN’ protocol and features multi-range control system coverage enabling us to identify and fix faults with ABS, SRS, ETS, climate control, ECM, immobiliser and instrument cluster systems, among many more. It also supports extended functions such as activation, reset basic settings, adaptation, repair guide etc
